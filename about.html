I'm Yusuf.

A half Finnish half American upper-secondary school student based in Southern Finland with a passion for multimedia production, the aerospace industry, and building stuff. 

In the past 3 years that I've tossed and dragged my reliable Canon gear around, I've had the opportunity to capture all sorts of industries. Aviation, events, and landscapes have been the most prominent, but I've stepped my foot into the doors of niches such as product photography as well.

While photos have been the most apparent, I do share creative videos/short films once in a while.
